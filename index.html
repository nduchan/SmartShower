Inspiration

SmartShower was inspired by the problem that every day we have to figure out who is going to take a shower and when. Imagine 6 people living in one house...and only a one shower. No one wants a headache due to shower scheduling headaches! We created SmartShower to make scheduling hassle-free, ensure people are mindful of the length of their showers, and apply an internet-of-things solution to an everyday problem we are faced with.

What it does

SmartShower generates an optimal shower schedule based on the Google Calendars of people who live together. Users access the web application to… *authorize SmartShower to access their Google Calendar *configure preferences such as preferred shower time *set a desired shower length prior to first event of the day *

Using a raspberry pi microcontroller, we made SmartShower not just a web application but also an internet-of-things device. With the SmartShower device users are… *informed about current shower status (scheduled, unscheduled) *given warning indicators regarding amount of time remaining (LED lights, sounds) *charged money for every minute past the scheduled end time
